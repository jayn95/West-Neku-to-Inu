About Us

West Neko to Inu was founded with the mission of improving the lives of stray animals
that call our university campus home. We believe every animal deserves a chance at a happy
and healthy life.

Raising Awareness

We strive to educate the university community about the challenges faced by stray animals on campus, fostering compassion and understanding.

Supporting Animal Welfare

We collaborate with local shelters and rescue organizations to provide necessary resources and medical care for the stray animals.

Finding Forever Homes

By sharing the stories and personalities of these animals, we hope to connect them with loving forever families.
